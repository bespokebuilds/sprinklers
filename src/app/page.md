## What is sprinkler.wiki?

This is an open-source knowledge base built by and for fire protection professionals. It covers everything from basic fire dynamics to advanced storage protection design, from estimation and bidding to project closeout.

Whether you are a new designer studying for NICET Level I or a senior technician looking up an obstruction rule, the goal is to make the information you need **findable, accurate, and practical**.

---

## How to use this site

The navigation on the left organizes content by topic area. Start with the **Introduction** section if you are new to the industry, or jump directly to the section you need.

**For new designers:**

- Start with [Fire Dynamics](/docs/fire-dynamics) and [Suppression Principles](/docs/suppression-and-prevention) to understand why sprinkler systems work
- Read [Industry Perspectives](/docs/perspectives) to understand the different roles in the trade
- Work through [System Types](/docs/system-specifics), [General Considerations](/docs/general-considerations), and [Hydraulic Calculations](/docs/hydraulic-calculations) to build core design skills
- Study the [NICET Certification](/docs/nicet) page when you are ready to pursue credentials

**For experienced designers:**

- Use the [Codes and Standards](/docs/communication) page as a quick reference for which standards apply
- Reference [Industry-Specific Requirements](/docs/industry-requirements) for specialized occupancies
- Check [Special Situations](/docs/special-situations) for unusual design conditions
- Review [Fire Pumps](/docs/pumps) and [Water Storage Tanks](/docs/water-tanks) for supply-side design

**For estimators and project managers:**

- [Estimation and Bidding](/docs/design) covers takeoff methods, labor units, and pricing
- [Project Management](/docs/project-management) covers the full project lifecycle
- [Pipe and Fittings](/docs/pipe-and-fittings) includes material pricing reference data
- [Fabrication](/docs/fabrication) and [Installation](/docs/installation) cover the build process
